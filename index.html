About Me

I received my B.Eng degree from Zhejiang University in 2013, and the PhD degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2018. From 2017 to 2018, I was with Alibaba group as an Algorithm Engineer, making contributions to the data-driven resource allocation for Alibaba's largest smart warehouse. Since Nov. 2018, I joined Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data as a Research Associate, directed by Prof. Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo and Prof. Shuguang Cui.

My research interests include:

  • Tensor Data Analytics and Applications

  • Bayesian Modeling and Inference

  • Machine Learning for Information System

Find out more in my Publications and Research Grants.
